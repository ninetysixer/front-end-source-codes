About Me

Hi, I'm Semih, a passionate self-taught developer based in Istanbul. I'm 26 years old and have been on a journey of self-education for the past year. My focus areas include Python, C++, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy exploring different technologies and continuously expanding my knowledge.

Apart from programming, I also have a keen interest in game development. Over the last six months, I have been honing my skills in Unity, where I have been developing my own games. It's an exciting process that allows me to combine my creativity with technical skills.

I believe in the power of technology to make a positive impact and solve real-world problems. I am always eager to take on new challenges, learn from experiences, and collaborate with like-minded individuals. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
